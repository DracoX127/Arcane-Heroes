Arcane Heroes — Gameplay
Arcane Heroes is a terminal RPG where u build and lead a team of elemental heroes as a commander. Recruit heroes, gear them up, fight monsters, complete quests, and grow stronger through leveling and skill trees to become the most powerful commander in the arcane world.

⚔️ Commander System

Pick 1 of 6 elemental commanders at the start
Each commander gives ur whole team a unique elemental ATK buff
Pyronis boosts Fire, Aquaryn boosts Water, Terradon boosts Earth, Zephyros boosts Air, Voltaris boosts Electric, Noctyra boosts Shadow

🦸 Hero Collection

Recruit heroes to build ur team roster
Each hero has their own unique abilities and elemental affinities
Heroes synergize with ur commander's element for extra power

🗡️ Turn-Based Combat

Battles are turn-based, u take turns attacking monsters
Stats like ATK, DEF, HP, SPD, and CRIT all matter in fights
Elemental matchups play a big role in dealing extra damage

📈 Progression

Earn EXP from battles and quests to level up
Unlock upgrades and new abilities through the skill tree
Stats grow stronger the higher ur level gets

🛒 Shop & Inventory

Spend Gold earned from battles and quests in the shop
Buy items and equipment to boost ur heroes
Equip gear to specific heroes to maximize their stats

📜 Quests

Take on quests with multiple objectives
Complete them to earn Gold, EXP, and rare rewards
Quests get harder as u progress through the game
